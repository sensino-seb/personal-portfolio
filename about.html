Facts about myself

Sebastian Vogeltanz

I build fullstack applications mainly with React and Javascript.

I studied Business Management and graduated in 2016. After working as a financial auditor and doing lots of work in Excel I started digging into Macros and Visual Basic to automate my work. I decided to do some online courses and learn Javascript from Jonas Schmedtmann and Python, Databases and API's from Coding Nomands. I like the logical side of programming and how everything finally fits together. I decided to do a conversion Master of Science in Data Engineering at Edinburgh Napier University which I graduated from in 2021 as one of the first graduates in this programme ever. I now work as a freelance developer and work my way towards fullstack development with Javascript and deployment in the cloud. If you'd like to do a project with me or need consultation don't hesitate to reach out to me. I am looking forward to working with you!
